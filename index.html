Have a question? Check out the FAQ

What is an online voting system?

An online voting system is a digital platform that allows voters to cast their votes securely and conveniently using the internet, ensuring accessibility, transparency, and privacy.

How secure is the online voting system?

Our platform uses advanced encryption, secure authentication methods, and tamper-proof technologies like blockchain to protect voter data and ensure vote integrity.

Can I trust that my vote will remain anonymous?

Yes, voter anonymity is guaranteed. Our system uses secure encryption to unlink your identity from your vote, ensuring complete privacy.

Who can use the online voting system?

The system is designed for a wide range of elections, including government elections, corporate voting, educational institutions, and organizational decision-making processes.

Is the system accessible for individuals with disabilities?

Absolutely! Our platform complies with accessibility standards (WCAG) and includes features like screen reader support, keyboard navigation, and multilingual options.

How do I know my vote was counted?

After you cast your vote, you’ll receive a confirmation. Additionally, our vote verification tools allow you to confirm your vote was recorded without compromising anonymity.
